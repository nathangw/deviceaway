Why Children and Teenagers Should Sleep Without Phones

In today's technology-driven world, the importance of sleep often gets overlooked, particularly among children and teenagers who are constantly connected to their smartphones. However, sleep is crucial for healthy growth, cognitive development, emotional regulation, and overall well-being. Encouraging children and teens to sleep without their phones is not just a good idea - its essential for their physical and mental health. DeviceAway™ is designed to help parents create healthier bedtime routines by ensuring that phones are not in the bedroom at night. 

How it Works

DeviceAway™ ​sends an email when the phone is removed from the charger. This feature can alert parents if the phone is taken off the charger during the night, indicating potential usage that could disrupt sleep. This real-time information allows parents to encourage proper phone use, ensuring that their children and teenagers stick to healthy routines.

Common Questions

Does it work with all devices?

Yes, as long as the device charges with any of the USB standards and requires less than 24 volts.   USB is preferred because it will charge faster.

Will it work with a laptop?

Yes, as long as the laptop charges with a USB C less than 24 volts.   The 16-inch MacBook Pro/Max WILL NOT WORK and should be avoided.

What if my child has many devices?

Use a multi output USB C wall adapter and connect multiple DeviceAway™ boards to each charging cable.   You can choose unique names for each device in Setup.

Can it send texts instead of emails?

Sending texts would incur a cost of around 5 cents per message and would require a monthly service plan instead of free emails. If you wish to use text notifications, contact support to explore setting up DeviceAway to send texts through your own account.

How long is the Warranty?

The product comes with a 1-year warranty from the date of purchase for normal usage. This does not cover water damage or damage caused by pets (such as dog mauling).
