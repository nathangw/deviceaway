DeviceAway™ Setup

Follow these quick steps to start receiving email notifications.

Step 1:  Plug the DeviceAway™ side labeled "Wall" into a USB wall adapter and plug a phone, wireless charger or laptop to the “Device” side.  

Step 2:  Press the Setup button on back side of DeviceAway™ . The LED light will turn on, indicating that the Wi-Fi portal is active.  

Step 3:  On your phone, navigate to the Wi-Fi settings and click on the DeviceAway_Setup network. This process is the same for both Android and iPhone devices.

Step 4: Click on Setup to proceed. Choose your wifi network and enter your password. Also, enter the email where DeviceAway™ will send the emails to. The recipient email address is never shared and only stored locally.
