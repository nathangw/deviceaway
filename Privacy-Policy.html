Privacy Policy

We collect the information you submit via our contact form (name, email, message) to respond to your inquiry and provide support.

What we collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

How we use your info

  • Reply to questions and provide support
  • Follow up on product issues and feedback

Where it’s stored / processed

Your message is collected via Google Forms/Google Sheets and is emailed to support@deviceaway.net (handled by our email provider).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers needed to run the form and email (e.g., Google).

Retention

We keep messages for 12 months unless you ask us to delete them sooner.

Your choices

To access, update, or delete your message, contact support@deviceaway.net.

Children

The contact form is intended for adults/parents. If a child under 13 submits information, please contact us to remove it.
