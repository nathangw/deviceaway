The Importance of Sleep and Why Children and Teenagers Should Sleep Without Phones

 

In today's technology-driven world, the importance of sleep often gets overlooked, particularly among children and teenagers who are constantly connected to their smartphones. However, sleep is crucial for healthy growth, cognitive development, emotional regulation, and overall well-being. Encouraging children and teens to sleep without their phones is not just a good idea - its essential for their physical and mental health.

 

DeviceAway is designed to help parents create healthier bedtime routines by ensuring that phones are not in the bedroom at night. Here's how it works:

 

 

 

Here's how it works:

 

Phone Charging Alerts: DeviceAway can notify parents via email when the phone is plugged into the charger. This allows parents to verify that the phone is being charged in a central location outside the bedroom, promoting better sleep hygiene. By knowing that the phone is charging outside the room, children and teens will be less tempted to use it late into the night.

 

Phone Removal Alerts: DeviceAway also sends an email when the phone is removed from the charger. This feature can alert parents if the phone is taken off the charger during the night, indicating potential usage that could disrupt sleep. This real-time information allows parents to encourage proper phone use, ensuring that their children and teenagers stick to healthy routines.

 

 

With no apps, no fees, and a Wi-Fi portal that works on any smartphone, setting up DeviceAway is fast and intuitive. You simply connect to the Wi-Fi portal, enter your email address, and all the backend email services are automatically managed. Whether the phone charges via a cable or wirelessly, DeviceAway will ensure you're kept informed with real-time notifications.
