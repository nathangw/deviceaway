About

 

 

 

DeviceAway&#8482; is a family-owned business based in the United States. My name is Nathan Gwozdecki, and I founded this company to address a personal need within my family - managing my children's phone usage, especially during nighttime.

 

For years, I tried to encourage my children to put their phones down at night to improve their sleep. I experimented with many different parental control apps, but they always found ways to prioritize their phones over getting a good night's rest. That is when I built DeviceAway&#8482;, and a simple solution to a persistent problem was found - getting the phones out of the bedroom and into the hallway at night.
